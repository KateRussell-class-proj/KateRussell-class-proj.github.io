Here are some fun facts about Eastern Bluebirds!

  • Eastern Bluebirds can be found all across Eastern North America and as far south as Nicaragua!
  • The diet of the Eastern Bluebird consits mainly of insects and fruits, but they have been documented occasionally snacking on lizards, salamanders, mice, and other small vertebrates. Yikes!
  • Eastern Bluebirds often produce several successful broods a year! Young hatched early in the season leave the nest over the summer, but young that is hatched
    later may stay with their parents over the winter.
